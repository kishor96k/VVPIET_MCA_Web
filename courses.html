Courses Offered

College, We offer a variety of programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their academic and professional careers. Whether you’re starting your journey with us in pursuing advanced degrees in Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A.), Master of Computer Applications (M.C.A.) (Computer Science), We have specific programs for you.

B.C.A

Bachelor of Computer Applications

3 Year undergraduate program (Under AICTE)

Know more About BCA

B.C.A. Program Highlights

  • Well-Rounded Curriculum: Our B.C.A. program features a well-rounded curriculum that covers key areas such as programming languages, software engineering, database management, networking and web development. The coursework is designed to provide both fundamental and advanced knowledge in computer science.
  • Hands-On Learning: Emphasis is placed on practical experience through lab work, projects and real-world applications. Students gain valuable insights and hands-on experience with the latest technologies and industry-standard tools.
  • Industry-Relevant Skills: The program focuses on developing critical technical skills and problem-solving abilities, preparing graduates for various roles in the IT industry. Topics include software development, system analysis and cybersecurity.
  • Experienced Faculty: Our program is delivered by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to providing quality education and mentorship. They bring a wealth of industry experience and academic expertise to the classroom.
  • Career Opportunities: Graduates of the B.C.A. program are well-prepared for careers in software development, IT consulting, system administration and more. The program also provides a strong foundation for pursuing advanced studies in computer science or related fields.

M.C.A

Master of Computer Applications

2 Year Postgraduate Program (Under AICTE)

Know more About MCA

M.C.A. Program Highlights

  • Introduction: MCA is a two-year full-time Post Graduate Programme approved by AICTE. The course is designed to provide advanced knowledge in computer applications,software development, data science, networking and emerging technologies. It aims to prepare students for leadership roles in the IT industry.
  • Duration: 2 Years, Full-Time (AICTE Approved)
  • Eligibility: Graduation in relevant discipline with required entrance criteria
  • Focus Areas: Advanced Computer Applications, Software Development, Data Science, Networking, and Emerging Technologies
  • Learning Approach: Classroom teaching, practical labs, projects, seminars and industry exposure
  • Career Opportunities: Software Developer, System Analyst, Data Scientist, IT Consultant, Researcher, and more
  • Extra Edge: Placement support, skill development courses and hands-on training with modern tools
  • Scope of Placement: The MCA program opens doors to a wide range of career opportunities in the IT industry, including roles such as: Software Developer / Engineer, System Analyst, Database Administrator, Web & Mobile Application Developer, Data Scientist / AI Specialist, IT Consultant.
